Upcoming and past events

American Academy of Advertising Annual Conference

2019 Annual Conference
March 28-31

The American Academy of Advertising Annual Conference is a four-day event focused on how to approach advertising in our changing world. Breakouts into special topics sessions along with exhibitors and receptions will allow attendees to network, learn more about the advertising climate, and develop an understanding of how to work with advertising. Attendees also have the opportunity to submit competitive research papers or special topics of their own to be considered for various prestigious awards. Member registration costs $365, student member registration costs $245.

American Academy of Advertising Global Conference

2018 Global Conference - TBA
Location and date TBA

AAA's Global Conference will serve as an important forum for intellectual exchange and cross-national collaboration among advertising scholars, educators, and practitioners from around the world.We invite special topic session proposals and original research papers investigating any aspect of advertising. Although we welcome submissions on all advertising-related topics, papers and proposals focusing on the questions, challenges, and opportunities arising from the evolution of advertising driven by the technological and cultural forces around the world are particularly welcome.
